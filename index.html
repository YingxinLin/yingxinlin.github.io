Biography

I am a PhD candidate in Statistics under the supervision of Prof. Jean Yang and Dr. John Ormerod at the University of Sydney. I am a member of the School of Mathematics and Statistics and Sydney Bioinformatics and Biometrics Group. My main research interest is in normalisation and statistical modelling of single-cell RNA-seq data.
