Biography

I am a PhD candidate in Statistics under the supervision of Prof. Jean Yang, Dr. Rachel Wang and Dr. John Ormerod at the University of Sydney. I am a member of the School of Mathematics and Statistics and Sydney Precision Bioinformatics. My main research interest is in integrative analysis and statistical modelling of single-cell omics data.
