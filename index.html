Biography

I am a PhD candidate in Statistics under the supervision of Prof. Jean Yang, Dr. John Ormerod and Dr. Rachel Wang at the University of Sydney. I am a member of the School of Mathematics and Statistics and Sydney Bioinformatics and Biometrics Group. My main research interest is in normalisation and statistical modelling of single-cell RNA-seq data.
